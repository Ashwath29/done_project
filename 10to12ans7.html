In arithmetic, the least common multiple or LCM of two numbers say a and b, is denoted as LCM (a,b). The LCM is the smallest or least positive integer that is divisible by both a and b. The Least Common Multiple (LCM) is a way of finding the smallest common multiple between any two numbers or more. A common multiple is a multiple of two numbers or more. LCM denotes the less common or multiple factors of any two or more given integers.
