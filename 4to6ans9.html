(b) Perimeter of an equilateral triangle = 30 cm Side = ? Perimeter of equilateral triangle = 3 × side ⇒ 30 = 3 × side ⇒ Side = 30 / 3 = 10 cm Therefore, 10 cm is the length of each side of an equilateral triangle.
